Origins

Where It All Started

The Neuroscience Foundation

Neuroplastic Labs was born from a simple observation: the tools we use to measure cognitive and motor performance haven't kept pace with our understanding of the brain. Founded by Bryson Rhodes, a neuroscientist and athlete who saw firsthand the gap between lab-grade insights and everyday accessibility, the company set out to democratize brain data.

Drawing from research in neuroplasticity, the brain's ability to rewire and adapt; We built our prototype of the Neuroplastic Fitness Index (NFI), a holistic scoring system that maps cognitive-motor performance in real time.

From Lab to Product

What started as a research project quickly evolved into a product vision. We realized that the same AI and computer vision technology powering our cognitive assessments could be applied to entirely different domains, from Plant Health Screening to Analyzing Parkinson's Gait.

In 2025, we launched Plant Masters, our first consumer application, proving that deep scientific methodology could be packaged into beautiful, accessible mobile experiences. The NFI platform followed, bringing our core brain-body measurement technology to athletes, coaches, and clinicians.

The Vision

Building the Operating System for Human Performance

Today, Neuroplastic Labs operates at the intersection of neuroscience, AI, and consumer technology. Our team combines deep scientific expertise with world-class engineering to build products that make complex brain-body science accessible to everyone.

We believe that understanding your own neuroplastic potential isn't a luxury; it's a fundamental human right. Whether you're an elite athlete optimizing reaction time, a student sharpening focus, or someone recovering from injury, our technology meets you where you are.
